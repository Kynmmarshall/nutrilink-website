Why NutriLink

Built for equitable food redistribution

From Flutter clients to Node + PostgresSQL services, every layer supports verified food providers, transparent beneficiary requests, and measurable community impact.

Surplus listing + expiry guard

Providers specify quantity, pickup windows, and expiry; NutriLink auto-flags stale posts so only safe food moves forward.

Beneficiary discovery

Households browse nearby listings, filter by dietary need, and submit requests with status tracking and notifications.

Delivery orchestration

Delivery agents claim tasks, receive optimized pickup/drop details, and confirm completion for admins and providers.

Admin + impact analytics

Moderation tools, audit logs, and impact dashboards reveal meals delivered, waste diverted, and community reach.

User journeys

Every stakeholder has a guided flow

Prioritized user stories keep the MVP laser-focused on eliminating waste while creating dignity for households that rely on timely deliveries.

Food providers

Restaurants, grocers, and caterers list surplus items, receive collection confirmations, and monitor impact stats for CSR reporting.

"I want to list surplus food so it doesn’t go to waste."

Beneficiaries

Families discover nearby meals, request portions that match their needs, and follow delivery status without app-hopping.

"I want to see available food near me and track requests."

Delivery agents

Couriers accept jobs, access maps and instructions, and log proof-of-delivery to earn incomes while serving neighbors.

"I want clear pickup and drop-off details for every run."

Administrators

Moderators oversee listings, approve partners, and present real-time impact metrics to NGOs, city partners, and the Hult Prize jury.

"I want to monitor platform activity and impact data."

Live visibility

The community map keeps all four roles in sync

A shared map helps providers, beneficiaries, delivery agents, and admins coordinate pickups, drop-offs, and outreach. Real-time filters spotlight who needs support and where surplus is ready, so everyone can connect faster.
